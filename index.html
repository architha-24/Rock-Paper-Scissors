ROCK PAPER SCISSORS

How to Play :

  • Choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors by clicking a button.
  • The computer will also choose one randomly.
  • Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, Paper beats Rock.
  • If both choose the same, it's a tie.
  • Check your score anytime using the “Check Score” button.
  • Reset your score using “Reset Score” button.
  • Play multiple rounds to track your wins, losses, and ties.
